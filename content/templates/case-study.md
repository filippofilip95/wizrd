# Case Study Template

**Version**: 1.0

---

## How to Use This Template

1. Copy everything below the `## TEMPLATE` section
2. Fill in all `[PLACEHOLDER]` values
3. Delete sections that aren't relevant
4. Follow your brand voice - be direct, specific, no buzzwords
5. **Always include** "What Went Wrong" and "Lessons Learned" - honesty sells

---

## Checklist Before Publishing

### Must Have
- [ ] KPIs with numbers (before/after or specific values)
- [ ] Client pain points (what hurt)
- [ ] Project phases (how you approached it)
- [ ] Project management (team, communication, timeline)
- [ ] Real results with metrics
- [ ] Benefits (quantitative + qualitative)
- [ ] What we didn't know at the start (unknowns)
- [ ] What went wrong
- [ ] Lessons Learned

### Nice to Have
- [ ] ROI calculation
- [ ] Client quote/testimonial
- [ ] Tech stack
- [ ] Screenshots/visuals

### Brand Voice Check
- [ ] No buzzwords ("revolutionary", "game-changing", "cutting-edge")
- [ ] Specific numbers instead of vague claims
- [ ] Problems and failures acknowledged
- [ ] Practical lessons, not theory

## Anti-Patterns (What to Avoid)

### ❌ Vague Claims
- "Significantly improved efficiency"
- "Revolutionary solution"
- "Best-in-class results"

### ✅ Specific Numbers
- "Reduced time from 30 minutes to 2 minutes (-93%)"
- "300+ hours saved monthly"
- "4.5x annual ROI"

### ❌ Hiding Problems
- "Project went smoothly"
- "Everything worked as planned"

### ✅ Transparency
- "First version of filters didn't work - we had to rebuild them"
- "We started too complicated - should have focused on 1 thing"

### ❌ Generic Benefits
- "Improved productivity"
- "Better user experience"

### ✅ Specific Impact
- "Staff finds results in 2 minutes instead of 30"
- "New employee has access to all contacts from day one"
